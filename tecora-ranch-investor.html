  • Fast Format: Every accessible public course is 18 holes requiring 4–5 hours. Tecora Ranch is playable in under 2 hours — the only such option in the market.

  • Primary: Working professionals ages 30–55 along the I-85 corridor seeking a sub-2-hour round after work or on weekend mornings.
  • Secondary: Families with junior golfers — the shorter, forgiving executive format is ideal for introducing children to the game.
  • Tertiary: Retirees who play multiple times per week and prefer a casual, low-pressure environment over formal private club culture.
Common Questions

Founding Partner
Q & A

How can you build a 9-hole layout for just $355,000 in starting capital?

We are not building a traditional corporate course. Our 59.01 acres already has the natural terrain, meaning we are not paying to reshape the earth. By using a dirt exchange to get our 1.5-acre pond dug at no cost, selling our own timber to clear fairways, and building low-maintenance push-up greens from native soil, we reduce traditional construction costs by over 70 percent.

What happens if a major rainstorm damages the course?

Risk has been engineered out of the layout. We have zero sand bunkers — natural grass depressions cannot wash out. Our most flood-prone acreage along Kings Creek is locked inside a permanent Conservation Easement. The playing corridors sit on the high northern plateau. An SBA 504 line of credit serves as a commercial safety net for operational cash flow without touching the partners' capital.

Does the Conservation Easement hurt the resale value or block future expansion?

No — it protects the core asset. The easement covers 34 acres of steep ridges and creek buffers that are unbuildable under any circumstance. The explicitly carved-out 25-acre Build Zone remains 100 percent free of easement restrictions, giving full room for 9 holes, a driving range, and the clubhouse. The easement delivers $112,500 in upfront capital without surrendering a single square foot of the playing arena.

How does a 9-hole executive course compete with established 18-hole clubs?

We are not competing with them — we are capturing the market they are losing. Traditional 18-hole courses require 5 hours and thousands in annual dues. Modern golfers along the I-85 corridor want premium, fast golf playable in under 2 hours. Tecora Ranch provides that experience at a public green fee with no membership obligation.

What is the process for becoming a Founding Partner?

Step one is signing and returning the Letter of Intent on this page. Step two is completing a brief Accredited Investor Questionnaire confirming eligibility under SEC Rule 501. Step three is reviewing and executing the Subscription Agreement. No funds are transferred until both parties have signed and accredited investor status is verified.
