The Structure

You own it. You don't run it.

You are PropCo, you own the building and the license. The license attaches at the location. Getting it issued is what converts a vacant property into an operating healthcare asset.

The OpCo runs the census, the staff, and the care. You capture the asset appreciation, the license is what makes it possible.

The Choice

Two ways to hold it. Both capture the appreciation.

Passive

Lease to an Operator

The OpCo runs the census, the staff, the care. You collect rent on a healthcare-grade tenant in a building that now has a licensed use.

Active

Hire an Administrator

You keep the operating margin and pay a professional to run it, capturing both the cash flow and the appreciation.
