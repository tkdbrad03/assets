2Week
Licensing
Mississippi Licensing Requirements & Compliance Roadmap
⌄
Session Topics
  • Mississippi Department of Health licensing process for adult day care
  • Required application documents, facility layout, and staffing plan
  • Health and safety inspection requirements and how to prepare
  • Staff-to-client ratios required by Mississippi
  • ADA compliance requirements for your facility
  • Zoning and land use permits for Tylertown area
Your Action Items
  • Download and review the Mississippi adult day care license application
  • Begin drafting your proposed staffing plan
  • Confirm zoning eligibility for prospective facility locations

3Week
Medicaid Strategy
Medicaid Provider Application, Positioning & Bridge Funding
⌄
Session Topics
  • How Mississippi Medicaid waiver programs work for adult day care
  • Step-by-step walkthrough of the Medicaid provider application process
  • Documentation and record-keeping standards required for Medicaid billing
  • Realistic approval timelines and what to do in the interim
  • Bridge funding: grants, local aging services, and ACL programs in Mississippi
  • How to position your existing client relationships to strengthen your application
Your Action Items
  • Begin Medicaid provider enrollment with Mississippi Division of Medicaid
  • Compile documentation for each of your existing clients
  • Apply for at least two bridge grant opportunities identified in session
  • Set up a basic record-keeping system for client documentation
Specific to You Your existing clients are already Medicaid recipients. The goal of this session is to get you positioned to begin billing for the care you are already providing as quickly as possible.
4Week
Facility Strategy
Finding, Financing & Setting Up Your Space
⌄
Session Topics
  • Mississippi facility space requirements: square footage, layout, and safety
  • How to evaluate prospective locations in a rural market like Tylertown
  • Creative financing strategies for securing a space without large upfront capital
  • Lease negotiation tactics and what to look for in a commercial lease
  • Renovation priorities to meet licensing requirements efficiently
  • Essential equipment and supplies and how to source them cost-effectively
Your Action Items
  • Identify two to three candidate locations in or near Tylertown
  • Review each location against the Mississippi facility compliance checklist
  • Get at least one lease reviewed before signing anything
  • Create a prioritized renovation and equipment list with estimated costs
Specific to You As a real estate investor since 2018 specializing in negotiations and creative financing, Dr. McCray will bring direct expertise to this session. The building does not have to be a barrier.
5Week
Operations

⌄

  • Day-to-day operations framework: scheduling, care delivery, meal planning
  • Billing infrastructure setup for Medicaid reimbursement

  • Execute signed service agreements with all current clients
  • Complete a care plan template for each client
  • Post job descriptions and begin hiring process if additional staff are needed
  • Set up your billing and scheduling software (tools recommended in session)
Specific to You You are already spending most of your day with these clients. This session turns what you are doing informally into a documented, compliant, and billable operation.
