Admissions Process
Simple Steps to
Getting Started
01
Initial Inquiry
Reach out by phone, form, or walk-in. Our admissions team will answer every question and help determine if MsQue is the right fit.
02
Schedule a Tour
Visit our center, meet the team, and see our programs in action. We encourage you to bring your loved one so they feel comfortable from day one.
03
Care Assessment
Our clinical team conducts a personalized assessment to understand your loved one's needs, preferences, and goals — creating a tailored care plan.
04
Insurance & Enrollment
We accept Medicaid, private pay, and most major insurance. Our team guides you through every form and handles authorization on your behalf.
05
Welcome Day
Your loved one joins our community with a warm, structured welcome — ensuring they feel at home from the very first morning.
06
Ongoing Partnership
We communicate with families regularly — sharing updates, progress, and care plan adjustments so you're always in the loop.

Questions about eligibility? Most adults 18+ who need supervision or assistance with daily activities qualify. Medicaid waiver programs may cover all or part of the cost. Call us — we'll walk you through everything at no obligation.
