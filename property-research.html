Facility Strategy — How You Secure Your Location
1

Church / Community Partnership (Primary Strategy)

This is your fastest path to opening. No credit required. Churches often have unused weekday space and are aligned with your mission to serve the community.

✔ No credit barrier
✔ Low or flexible monthly cost
✔ Faster approval pathway

Action: Begin outreach to 3–5 local churches this week.
View / Download Church Partnership Proposal
2

Private Landlord Lease (No Credit Check)

Focus on small local property owners. Your value is predictable Medicaid revenue, not your credit score.

✔ Target off-market or locally owned buildings
✔ Lead with your 14–17 existing clients
✔ Emphasize consistent monthly payments
3

Seller Financing / Land Contract

Negotiate directly with the property owner. No bank required. Payments go straight to the seller.

✔ No traditional loan approval
✔ Flexible terms
✔ Common in rural Mississippi markets
4

Advanced Options (Use If Needed)

Subject-To purchases or equity partnerships can work, but require stronger negotiation and structure.

✔ Subject-To (take over existing mortgage)
✔ Revenue share with property owner
✔ Use only if primary paths do not close

Action Plan and Zoning Strategy
1

Contact Doug Rushing Realty

They manage several of these listings and have direct experience navigating Tylertown's local zoning boards and property variances. Mention you are opening an adult day care and need to understand zoning options before committing.

Phone: (601) 876-3409
2

Obtain a Zoning Verification Letter Before Any Purchase

This document provides legal certainty that the property can be used for adult day care. Required for your Mississippi DOM license application and for any future lenders or grant applications.

File at: Tylertown City Hall, 212 Beulah Avenue  |  Phone: (601) 876-4011
3

Contact Walthall County Economic Development Authority

Ask about available commercial buildings not yet listed, healthcare business incentives, and any county interest in supporting adult day care development in the area.

Phone: (601) 876-3021
4

Do Not Sign Any Lease or Purchase Agreement Until

You have received a Zoning Verification Letter confirming adult day care is permitted or can be permitted at that specific address. Mississippi DOM requires this before approving your facility.

Zoning Verification Request Template
Subject: Request for Zoning Verification – [Insert Property Address] To the Zoning Administrator, I am requesting a Zoning Verification Letter for the property at [Insert Address]. I intend to operate a 20-client Adult Day Care Center at this location under the Mississippi Medicaid Elderly and Disabled Waiver program. I wish to verify the following: 1. The current Zoning Classification of this property (e.g., C-1, Office, or Residential). 2. Whether Adult Day Care is a Permitted Use or requires a Special Exception or Conditional Use Permit. 3. Any specific parking, signage, or accessibility requirements applicable to this use. 4. Whether there are any active Conditional Use Permits or restrictions on record for this property. Please advise on the applicable fee and estimated processing time for this verification. Thank you for your assistance. [Your Name] [Your Business Name] [Your Phone Number] [Your Email Address]
