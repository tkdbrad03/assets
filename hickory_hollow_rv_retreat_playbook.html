Bottom line
No zoning means no waiting. Get your gravel quote, confirm the SC business license situation, then break ground. Fill two monthly anchors first — that's your stable base. Open the remaining sites nightly and weekly to test demand. Realistic payback is 7–9 months at 50% occupancy. That's a strong play with a name that sounds like a real destination.
