Opening

Hi, my name is LaTonya. I am a nurse here in Tylertown and I am opening a licensed adult day care for seniors and adults with disabilities.

Credibility

I already have 14 to 17 individuals who will be enrolling and I am currently working through licensing and Medicaid approval.

The Ask

I am looking to partner with a church that has unused weekday space and would be open to a lease arrangement that also creates steady income for the church while serving the community.

Close

Who would be the best person to speak with about something like this?

If They Ask What This Is

It is daytime care for seniors and adults with disabilities. It allows families to keep their loved ones at home instead of placing them in a facility.

If They Ask How It Works

It would be weekday use only — Monday through Friday during the day. The church keeps full use of the space evenings and weekends.

If They Ask for More Info

Absolutely. I can send a formal proposal over. What is the best email address for you?
